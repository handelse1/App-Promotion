EatFreely is YOUR guide - by you and for you. We have been helping James Madison University students feel more comfortable and confident with their dining choices. View student reviews, set dietary filters, and compare dining locations. Get ready to feel the delight with every bite. Keep scrolling to learn more about our app and the three features that make it so unique.

1. Dietary Filters

Define which foods or ingredients you want to avoid when searching for food options. With our app, you can select your food preferences or restrictions in order to narrow your search and find only what you are looking for.

2. Compare Dining Locations

Easily discover which dining locations have what you are looking for. On the app, you are able to select certain dining areas on campus to help narrow your food search.

3. Student Reviews

View and leave your own reviews that include ratings and photos. These student ratings include information on, but not limited to, meals, service, dining and location.
